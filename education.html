Education

Here's what I've learned

I have been here at BYU studying finance, and I have learned so much knowledge about the finance world and how it operates. The most important thing that I have gained from all of it is my desire to share that knowledge with others and help better their lives because of it.

This is one of main reasons I have decided to study finance. The idea that one day I will be able to help people deal with their finances and get them out of situations that make them stressed makes me so happy. I also enjoy finance because I enjoy learning about how the world works financially and how money makes the world go around. I also enjoy the analytical aspect of finance where one has to really understand concepts to figure different puzzles of upcoming future financial events. Neverthless, the thing that makes me so excited about finance is that I can help other people understand it as well and help them feel relieved about their future financially.

Ideally, with all of this education I have gained and will be gaining, I believe I will be a wise candidate to have on someone's team because of my sincere desire to help others. I am dilligent in my studies and try my best to really understand concepts so that I may be better prepared for a real-life job, specifically to be a financial advisor.
